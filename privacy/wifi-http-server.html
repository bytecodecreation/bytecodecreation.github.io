Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 24, 2026 | Last Updated: May 24, 2026

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for WiFi HTTP Server ("the App"), developed by Bytecode Creations ("we", "us", "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information that may be processed through your use of the App.

1. Information We Collect and Process

The App serves files over HTTP on your local Wi-Fi connection. We do not store or track what files you share, nor do we run a cloud server. Your data is directly accessible only by the clients you provide with your local IP address.

Permissions Used

  • Wi-Fi Connection Information: Needed to bind the local server socket to your current IP.
  • Storage Access: Required to serve user-selected directories to your web browser.

2. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

We do not track or share user activity. Third-party integrations are kept to a minimum:

  • Google Play Services: Used to verify the integrity and license of the App.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

All sharing records and logs are volatile and stored in local memory only. They are not stored on any remote servers.

4. Children's Privacy

This App does not request or collect personal information.

5. Security

We value your trust in providing us your information, and we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. All network transmissions initiated by the App are encrypted in transit using standard HTTPS protocols.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at: bytecode.creation@gmail.com.
