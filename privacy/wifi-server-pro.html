Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 24, 2026 | Last Updated: May 24, 2026

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for WiFi Server Pro ("the App"), developed by Bytecode Creations ("we", "us", "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information that may be processed through your use of the App.

1. Information We Collect and Process

The App is a local file sharing server that runs on your local network. No files, documents, user settings, or folder directories are uploaded to our servers. All file transfer operations occur entirely within your local WiFi network environment.

Permissions Used

  • Wi-Fi Connection Information: Required to obtain local IP addresses for hosting HTTP/FTP servers.
  • Storage (Photos/Videos/All Files): Required to list and share selected files over the hosted server.
  • Internet Access: Required to host the server port within your local network loop.

2. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

We do not integrate any analytical or tracking SDKs that share personal data. Standard crash reports might be processed anonymously through Google Play Services.

  • Google Play Services: Used for license verification and basic anonymous crash analytics.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

We do not host any remote databases. All connection logs and files remain exclusively on your physical device and are deleted when the App is uninstalled.

4. Children's Privacy

This App is a productivity tool and is not intended to target children under 13. We do not collect or request any personal identifiers.

5. Security

We value your trust in providing us your information, and we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. All network transmissions initiated by the App are encrypted in transit using standard HTTPS protocols.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at: bytecode.creation@gmail.com.
