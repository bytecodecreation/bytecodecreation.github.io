Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 24, 2026 | Last Updated: May 24, 2026

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for AI Voice Generator ("the App"), developed by Bytecode Creations ("we", "us", "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information that may be processed through your use of the App.

1. Information We Collect and Process

The App is primarily a tool that processes text and audio files locally on your device. We do not transmit your voice recordings, generated speech, or transcripts to any proprietary servers. However, to show advertising and facilitate in-app purchases, we integrate third-party services that collect device-level identifiers.

Permissions Used

  • Record Audio: Required to capture speech input for transcribing audio to text (STT). Voice processing is performed locally or handled securely by the device's system voice engine.
  • Internet Access: Required to display advertising and process Google Play In-App purchases.

2. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

We utilize third-party advertising and billing services inside this App. These partners may collect and share device identifiers (such as the Google Advertising ID):

  • Google Play Billing: Handles in-app purchases and subscriptions securely on behalf of Google Play.
  • Google AdMob: Serves personalized and non-personalized ads. Google Privacy Policy
  • InMobi Ads: Serves targeted advertising. InMobi collects and shares Device/other IDs for ad optimization and tracking. InMobi Privacy Policy

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Any user data (such as voice generator text history or settings) is stored locally on your device in shared preferences/local database storage. It is not saved on our servers. Third-party advertising IDs are handled and retained by Google and InMobi in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

4. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

5. Security

We value your trust in providing us your information, and we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. All network transmissions initiated by the App are encrypted in transit using standard HTTPS protocols.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at: bytecode.creation@gmail.com.
